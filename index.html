Welcome to Austin Scout Troop 123!

We are a dedicated Scout Troop based in Austin, Texas. Our mission is to instill the values of leadership, service, and outdoor adventure in young Scouts. Join us on our journey to make a positive impact in the community and the world.

We meet regularly to plan exciting activities like camping trips, community service projects, and leadership development events. Whether you're new to scouting or an experienced member, there's a place for you with us!

About Us

Austin Scout Troop 123 has been serving the youth of Austin for over 25 years. Our troop is dedicated to developing strong character and leadership in young individuals through the Scout Oath and Scout Law.

We are part of the larger Capitol Area Council, which provides us with access to numerous resources, events, and training opportunities to help our Scouts grow. Our leaders are committed volunteers who support Scouts in reaching their full potential in a fun and challenging environment.
